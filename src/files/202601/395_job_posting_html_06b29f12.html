      Base pay range

      $233,000.00/yr - $512,000.00/yr
      For our client, we are seeking a Vice President Engineering And AI Innovations to join the team of a leader in the Consumer Technology space. You’ll build and maintain systems that power mission-critical Consumer Technology workflows. You’ll collaborate with product and peers to design clean, scalable solutions. This role values craftsmanship, impact, and ownership.

      Location: Remote - US based candidates only, no visa sponsorship available

      Compensation: $233,000 – $512,000 annually.

      Responsibilities

      • Lead and mentor a team of software developers focused on AI Innovations
      • Direct the evolution and feature expansion of AI Insights and Agent Assist products
      • Oversee the integration of cutting-edge AI technologies into product capabilities
      • Develop and execute long-term engineering strategies in alignment with company goals
      • Ensure operational excellence through adherence to SRE principles for AI systems

      Qualifications

      • 10+ years experience in engineering management
      • Proven track record in leading AI product engineering delivery
      • Deep understanding of modern AI architectures like LLMs and RAG systems
      • Expert proficiency in Java and JVM-based ecosystems
      • Experience managing highly scalable, low-latency systems in a cloud environment

      Benefits

      • 100% coverage of employee health, dental, and vision insurance from day one
      • Access to mental health support services for employees and dependents
      • Generous employee stock purchase plan
      • 12 weeks of paid parental leave, along with paid time off and volunteer hours

      Our client is an equal opportunity employer. We encourage you to apply even if you don’t meet every qualification—your background could be exactly what this team needs.
